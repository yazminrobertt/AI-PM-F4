1.	How does effective communication reduce project risks?
Effective communication reduces project risks by ensuring clear understanding of goals, roles, and expectations, helping to identify issues early and prevent misunderstandings or errors.

2.	Which stakeholder is most critical to your project’s success and why?
The most critical stakeholder is the project sponsor/client because they provide approval, resources, and direction, which directly influence the project’s success.
